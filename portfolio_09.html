Southern Comfort

(A still life for my grandmother Ma-Maw, who never met a stranger)

I grew up in Georgia, constantly being dressed up and taken to social events where I was told to “behave”. Although wearing a suit as a child was no fun, I find myself cherishing those formalities and wanting to recreate that atmosphere. I would love to have one of those ornaments from Ma-Maw’s living room and pretend I have a fancy drawing room to entertain my friends.
