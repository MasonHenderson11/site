About Mason

​​I am an artist, living and working in Boulder, CO. Most of my work is focused in clay, though I recently started working with wood. Making art is a dynamic way for me to apply my analytical mind to three-dimensional space. When I’m not in studio, you can find me trail running, rock climbing, and making pasta from scratch.

I received my undergraduate degree in geology​,​ worked as an environmental consultant,​ and recently completed my MBA focused in sustainability and marketing.​​
